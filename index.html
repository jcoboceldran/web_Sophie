01 ABOUT ME

I am Sophie Castillo, a Masters student currently attending the University of California Berkeley in the Environmental Engineering Program. My focus is in air pollution (specifical automotive emissions like Nitrogen Oxides, Black Carbon, and Particulate Matter) and climate change mitigation, however I have training/academic experience in the fields of water quality, remediation, energy systems, and environmental design. I am deeply passionate about finding ways to effectively minimize pollutant emissions to protect human health and the quality of our environment, but I aim to do so in a way that doesn't disrupt natural processes or the emotional and mental wellbeing of the targeted populations.
