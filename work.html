RECETAS Research Group, ISGlobal Barcelona

Research Assistant

June 2022 to August 2022

Funded by UROP, served as an undergraduate researcher/intern at the ISGlobal Health Center on a project titled Re-imagining Environments for Connection and Engagement: Testing Actions for Social Prescribing in Natural Spaces (RECETAS). Project analyzed the impacts of suggesting time/activities in nature (nature-based prescribing) on the feelings of loneliness and anxiety of city-dwellers. I conducted an in-depth scientific literature review to obtain publications we could use in our own research, assisted in the planning of the Urban Health Conference the group was responsible for hosting that year, and conducted a market analysis of digital solutions available to the public in order to create our own unique platform with the help of software engineers.
